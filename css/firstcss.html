Step to make the cake:-

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly coat a 10-inch cake pan with butter and dust with all-purpose flour.
  2. Separate egg yolks from egg whites.
  3. Beat egg whites until stiff peaks. Reserve.
  4. Sift the flour, baking powder into a large mixing bowl.
  5. Beat butter until light and fluffy, approximately 10 minutes. Add sugar and mix together until light in color. Add in the eggs yolks one at a time; keep mixing until well combined. Add milk and vanilla gradually and continue beating until the mixture is very creamy.
  6. Add dry ingredients (flour and baking powder) 1 cup at the time.
  7. Foil in egg whites just until combined.
  8. Pour batter into the prepared pan and bake until a wooden skewer inserted in the center comes out clean ‚Äî 30 to 35 minutes. Cool cake in the pan on a wire rack for 5 minutes.
  9. Un-mold and cool completely. Decorate as you like or enjoy as is.
Note : This recipe makes 1 rectangular 9x13 inch cake pan, or 1 round 10-inch cake pan, or 2 round 8 or 9-inch cake pan.
